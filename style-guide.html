Interactive Elements

In-text link

CSS selector:

.link-text

Sample code:

Want to know more? <a href="#" class="link-text">Click here</a> to learn more about me.

Rendered element:

Want to know more? Click here to learn more about me.

Navigation Link

CSS selector:

.button-nav

Sample code:

<a href="#" class="button-nav">Scroll to the Top</a>

Paragraphs

CSS selectors:

p.paragraph-body

Sample code:

<p class="paragraph-body">Foodie Buddy is a food-focused social app that lets users leave reviews of the restaurants and post them on the feed. The app incorporates the camera of the phone to let users take photos when creating a post; alternatively, the users can use the photos in the phone gallery to do so. The app also lets users search nearby restaurants with the built-in map. </p>

Rendered element:

Foodie Buddy is a food-focused social app that lets users leave reviews of the restaurants and post them on the feed. The app incorporates the camera of the phone to let users take photos when creating a post; alternatively, the users can use the photos in the phone gallery to do so. The app also lets users search nearby restaurants with the built-in map.
